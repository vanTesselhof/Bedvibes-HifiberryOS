Digital signal processor

HiFIBerryOS supports a variety of DSP controls as:

  • Speaker crossovers
  • Parametric equalizers
  • Speaker and room profiles
  • Sound profiles
  • Daisy-chaining multiple DSPs

These features require a DSP-equipped sound cards as the HiFiBerry DAC+ DSP or the Beocreate 4-channel-amplifier. HiFiBerryOS will also support the TOSLink input of these boards.
